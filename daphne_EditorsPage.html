A progressive profile form was made with the use of prompts to collect the user information. This process consists of 4 steps. The user profile (Step 1) consists of 5 prompts, as in name, surname, age, gender, and agree with privacy terms. As we are done entering information into the prompts, it displays the collected data. As we are done with the user profile prompts, the next button will lead to the other step prompts, which are volunteering tasks (Step 2), Qualification (Step 3), Availability and contact information (Step 4). A progress bar was also used to represent the completion of the profile.
